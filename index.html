About

As a student majoring in Computer Science, I am interested in Software Development and Web Development. In 2024, I earned my associate's degree at Broward College. While at BC, I joined Phi Theta Kappa, an honor society for community colleges. Currently, I am pursuing my bachelor's degree at Florida Atlantic University.

I am in many FAU student organizations, including WIECS, SWE, GWC, NSBE, and SHPE. I know many programming languages, including Python, C++, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have also participated in hackathons where I collaborated with others to create great programs. I aim to develop software and web projects that can benefit the community.
